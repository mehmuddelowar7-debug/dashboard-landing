Restaurant Analytics & Decision Dashboard

An independently built analytics system designed to explore restaurant performance, delivery efficiency, and revenue behavior using real-world food delivery data.

Open Interactive Dashboard View Implementation (GitHub)

Why this system exists

Food delivery platforms generate high-volume operational data, but raw data alone does not support decisions. This project was built to demonstrate how messy, real-world data can be transformed into structured insight that supports operational and revenue-focused decision-making.

What the dashboard explores

Revenue Concentration

Identifies how revenue is distributed across restaurants and where demand is concentrated.

Order Behavior

Analyzes basket size, order frequency, discount usage, and timing patterns.

Delivery Efficiency

Explores preparation time and rider wait signals that impact customer experience.

Operational Sensitivity

Examines how operational variables influence order value and revenue outcomes.

Analytical modeling
